About Us

Welcome to L&G Heating & Cooling! With over a decade of experience, we are your trusted experts in the installation, service, and repair of all HVAC products. Our mission is to provide families and businesses with top-quality heating and cooling solutions that fit their needs and budgets.

Founded by a dedicated professional with a passion for HVAC excellence, L&G Heating & Cooling has built a reputation for reliability, expertise, and customer satisfaction. Whether you need a quick repair, a complete system installation, or routine maintenance, we have the knowledge and tools to get the job done right.

Our team is equipped with a comprehensive inventory of parts and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure we can swiftly address any heating or cooling issue you may encounter. We are committed to providing energy-efficient solutions to enhance your comfort while reducing your energy costs.

At L&G Heating & Cooling, we stand behind the quality of our work with a satisfaction guarantee. We serve a wide range of areas and are always ready to help you with your HVAC needs. Contact us today to learn more or to get a free estimate.
